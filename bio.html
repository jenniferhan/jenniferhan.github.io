About Jennifer Han

Jennifer Han was born and raised in New Jersey, alongside her parents and two sisters. She eventually moved to the Boston area in 2017 to attend college. Jennifer is currently a sophomore at Tufts University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Entrepreneurial Leadership Studies. Her first experience with coding was in her senior year of high school when she took AP Computer Science. Now, pursing a Bachelor of Science in CS, Jennifer hopes to make the world of programming a more accessible and diverse field for anyone who wants to pursue it as a career.
